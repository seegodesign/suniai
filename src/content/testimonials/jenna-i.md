When I met Capri, the simplest way I could describe the sensation of my soul is with the words “**instant safety**”. It’s rare in this life to feel fully seen without the need for words and the two hours that followed transported me to an alternate universe. One where it was safe to let go, safe to express, and mostly safe to heal.

I’m now 22 weeks pregnant with a baby boy- the result of our very first embryo transfer after trying to conceive for years. I have traveled back to Kauai (from South Carolina) twice just in this calendar year to melt into Capri’s massage table. If that doesn’t speak to her magnificence.. I don’t know what does.

**She is a woman still rooted in the goodness most of the world struggles to find. You will feel it in her energy instantly and it allows you to go to the corners within yourself that we all too often avoid.**

I owe so much of my healing and the forgiveness I found within to our time together and to the presence I feel from her friendship even from afar.

She’s a jewel. And I’m jealous of all of you that are local to Kauai and can see her weekly.
